HOW TO START WITH DRAMA

first, signin

when you signed in succusfully, you will automotically go to the home page, but if the browser did not do it, here is the link:

/home

then, you can get started with create a new todo:

  • click on the plus button on bottom right of window
  • when you click, a window opens, you can set your new todo details here
  • the first input is your todo's name, write your todo's title here
  • the next input is for some notes that you want to memorize fofr next time (named description in app)
  • the next is steps, you can tap on new step button, then a step input creates
  • if you're all set, you can tap on the button named add it to add the todo

    • WARN: do not use po/d in inputs, if you used, the app does not show todos any more and you must delete your file

  • when you created the todo, it will be showed on todos card
  • to do a todo, tap on the checkbox on left, to undo it, double tap on the checkbox
  • to see todo's details, tap on todo card and it will be showed as a card

in the left top card:

  • the app will show your local time and date
  • your username will be showed on top of card
  • if you tap on settings button, you can do these thins:
    1. the "download your file" button, you can download your todos as a DRAMA.json file
    2. if you typed po/d and the app crashed or did not work good, you can click on this button, your activity will be deleted
    3. you can logout your account with this button and all of your datas will be deleted
    4. in next lines, you can see some colored buttons, you can set the app's theme with clicking on these buttons
    5. you can also switch between dark and light theme with the switch that is in window

in left bottom card:

  • you can see your share keys
  • if someone called you, it will be showed in there
  • if you tap on this window, you will go to the share centre, where you can share your moments with anyone else

and the right bottom card is the ideas that you add in your calls

DRAMA SHARE CENTRE

for go to this page, you can click on bottom left card in the homepage, here's also the link:

/sharing_centre (if you're not signed in, you will direct to the sign in page)

in sharing centre, you can see a big card:

  • in top, your username is written
  • in left of your username, that's a circle, if you tap on it, you can see your keys, and change app's themes
  • in right of header, that's a plus button that you can add a new contact, click on it
  • when you clicked, a card will be showed, here, you can type a user's username to add them to your contacts
  • if you want to add a contact with a third_key, click on the switch on top of the input, then type the id
  • when you succussfully added a contact, it will be a new contact at the page, to call him, tap on the buttons on right
